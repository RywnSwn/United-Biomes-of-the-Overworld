What Are Glow Berries?

Glow Berries are a bioluminescent fruit found naturally growing on cave vines throughout the Overworld. While they serve a legitimate purpose as a light source and minor food item, the U.B. Department of Public Health has confirmed that consumption produces notable psychoactive and physical effects in citizens.

As of the Overworld Substance Regulation Act, Glow Berries are classified as a Schedule II Controlled Luminescent Substance and are subject to regulation, tracking, and in certain cases, restriction.

📈

Documented Effects

The following effects have been documented by the U.B. National Institute of Biome Health (NIBH) following consumption of Glow Berries:

💯
Speed Enhancement

A noticeable increase in movement speed lasting approximately 30 seconds. Citizens have reported an irresistible urge to sprint.

💪
Strength Boost

Temporary increase in physical strength. Do not attempt to pick fights with Iron Golems while under the influence.

✨
Luminescent Glow

A faint green glow becomes visible to nearby players, making the user identifiable to law enforcement.

😄
Other Reported Effects

Uncontrollable urge to climb cave vines. Excessive friendliness toward Endermen. Forgetting where you put your bed.

Need Help?

If you or a fellow citizen is struggling with Glow Berry dependency, the United Biomes government is here to help. You are not alone in the cave.

U.B. National Recovery Hotline: 1-800-NO-BERRI

Hours: Sun up to Sun down, 7 days a week (closed during Phantom attacks)

All contacts are confidential. The U.B. does not share caller information with the Department of Defense or hostile mobs.
