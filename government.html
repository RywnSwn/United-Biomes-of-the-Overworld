Government of the United Biomes

The structure, branches, departments, and laws of the U.B.

🏛

Branches of Government

The United Biomes of the Overworld operates under three branches of government, each responsible for a distinct function of the state.

⚖️
Executive Branch
Office of the President

The Executive Branch is led by the President of the United Biomes. The President is responsible for enforcing national law, leading the government, overseeing all departments, and representing the United Biomes in diplomatic matters.

Active
⚖️
Judicial Branch
U.B. Federal Court

The Judicial Branch is responsible for interpreting U.B. law and resolving disputes between citizens and the state. The U.B. Federal Court will serve as the highest legal authority in the nation.

Planned

National Law

The following laws apply to all citizens, visa holders, and any individual present within U.B. territory. Violation of U.B. law may result in removal from U.B. territory, revocation of citizenship or visa, or other consequences as determined by the government.

§ 1
Protection of Property

Griefing, destruction, or unauthorized modification of citizen or government property within U.B. borders is strictly prohibited and classified as an act of terrorism against the state.

§ 2
Prohibition of Explosive Devices

The use, possession, or distribution of explosive devices of any kind, including TNT and end crystals, within U.B. borders is strictly prohibited.

§ 3
Prohibition of Illegal Substances

The use, possession, or distribution of illegal potions or potion drugs within U.B. borders is strictly prohibited.

§ 4
Visa and Border Compliance

Unauthorized entry into U.B. territory without a valid visa or citizenship is prohibited. All individuals entering U.B. territory must comply with border and embassy personnel.

§ 5
Embassy Inspections

All residents and citizens must comply with embassy personnel during official inspections. Failure to comply is considered a violation of U.B. law.

§ 6
Construction Authorization

Non-citizens may not construct structures within U.B. borders without prior government authorization. Unauthorized construction is subject to removal.

⚠ Notice: U.B. national law is subject to change at the discretion of the Executive Branch. Citizens and visa holders are responsible for staying informed of any updates.
