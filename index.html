Controlled Substances Advisory — Glow Berries

The U.B. Department of Public Health formally classifies Glow Berries as a Schedule II Controlled Luminescent Substance under the Overworld Substance Regulation Act. While not immediately lethal, Glow Berries are known to produce psychoactive effects including heightened speed, increased physical strength, and a general sense of glowing invincibility.

Citizens are reminded that while personal use is not a criminal offense under current U.B. law, trafficking, distribution within 50 blocks of a village, or use during official government proceedings is strictly prohibited and punishable by up to 3 days of community mining service.

⚠ Known Effects of Glow Berry Use:
  • Mild speed enhancement (Duration: approx. 30 seconds)
  • Temporary increase in physical strength
  • A faint luminescent glow visible to nearby players
  • Uncontrollable urge to climb cave vines

If you or someone you know is struggling with Glow Berry dependency, contact the U.B. National Recovery Hotline at 1-800-NO-BERRI. Help is available. You are not alone in the cave.
